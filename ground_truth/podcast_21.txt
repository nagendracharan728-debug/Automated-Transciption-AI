Morning routine kaisa hona chahiye taaki hum apne brain ko prime kar saken, stress free rah saken aur highly successful ban saken.

Sabse pehle hume brain waves samajhni hongi. Jab hum sote hain to brain delta state me hota hai. Sone se pehle theta me jata hai. Jab hum uthte hain to delta se theta, phir alpha aur phir beta me aate hain.

Lekin aajkal log alarm se achanak uthte hain aur seedhe beta state me chale jate hain. Yeh waisa hi hai jaise gaadi ko neutral se seedha fifth gear me daal diya jaye. System par strain aata hai.

Jo beech ka powerful phase hota hai, theta aur alpha, agar use consciously use kar liya jaye to woh bahut kaam ka hai.

Isliye subah sabse pehle paanch minute aankh band karke baithiye. Us state me jaiye. Apne auto suggestions dijiye. Visualization kijiye. Jo aaj karna hai, jo hafte me karna hai, jo mahine me karna hai, jo saal me karna hai, use morning me include kijiye.

Brain ke andar ek structure hota hai, suprachiasmatic nucleus, jo circadian rhythm ko set karta hai. Pehle log surya ko arghya dete the. Yeh sirf ritual nahi tha, balki body clock activate karne ka tareeka tha. Light signal milte hi system kickstart ho jata hai.

Lekin agar aap seedhe phone, stress aur notifications me chale gaye to aapne beta state aur stress dono badha diye.

Peak performers ki routines me ek cheez common hoti hai. Unke rituals powerful hote hain aur woh jo karte hain full attention ke saath karte hain. Unka morning ka pehla hour structured hota hai.

Isi ko simple tareeke se movers technique kehte hain.

M for meditation
O for oxygenation
V for visualization
E for exercise
R for reading something positive
S for scribing ya journaling

Iska matlab yeh nahi ki ek ghanta lagega. Paanch paanch minute bhi kaafi hain.

Visualization ki power bahut badi hai. Studies me athletes ko do groups me baanta gaya. Ek physical practice kar raha tha aur doosra sirf imagination me practice kar raha tha. Teen mahine baad dono ke brain patterns similar mile.

Log vision board bana lete hain par feeling nahi laate. Visualization practice se aata hai. Pehle aap mind me bante hain phir reality me bante hain.

Mental rehearsal se brain pehle hi scenario analysis kar leta hai aur stress kam hota hai. Thoda stress achha hota hai lekin zyada stress nervousness aur palpitations laata hai.

Ab phone ki baat karein. Subah uthte hi phone dekhna risky hai kyunki control aapke haath se nikal jata hai. Bahari duniya decide karti hai ki aap kya sochenge.

Brain ki teen layers hoti hain.

Brainstem jo survival functions control karta hai.
Limbic system jo emotions aur threat scanning karta hai.
Frontal cortex jo control, decisions aur long term thinking deta hai.

Zyada log limbic loop me phase rehte hain jahan fight, flight, fear aur freeze response hota hai. Amygdala threat scanner ki tarah kaam karta hai.

Peak performers frontal cortex activate karna seekh lete hain. Wahi emotional control, commitment aur choices ka center hai.

Exercise bhi important hai. Strength, coordination, balance aur endurance sab zaruri hain. Cross lateral movement brain growth ke liye achhe hote hain.

Recent research me hand grip strength ka connection neuronal health aur memory se bhi joda gaya hai.

Isliye morning routine aisa hona chahiye jo brain ko shock na de, body ko activate kare, mind ko direction de aur stress regulate kare.

Pehle mind me jeetiye, phir reality me jeetiye.
